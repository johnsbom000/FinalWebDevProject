Everything you need for a safe and fun day in Zion.

Essential Day Hiking Gear

  1. Navigation
    • Trail map (paper)
    • Fully charged phone
    • Offline maps (AllTrails, Gaia)
  2. Water & Hydration
    • 2–3 liters of water minimum
    • Electrolyte packets
  3. Food & Nutrition
    • Trail snacks
    • Sandwich or protein meal
  4. Safety
    • Basic first aid kit
    • Whistle
    • Sunscreen & lip balm
  5. Clothing
    • Breathable hiking shirt
    • Sturdy footwear with traction
    • Extra socks

Sample Day Hiking Itinerary

  1. 7:00 AM – Arrive at the park, park, and prep gear
  2. 7:30 AM – Start hike (Angels Landing or Emerald Pools)
  3. 10:00 AM – Snack break and hydration
  4. 12:00 PM – Lunch with scenic views
  5. 2:00 PM – Begin return hike
  6. 4:00 PM – Stretch, cool down, and rehydrate
  7. 5:00 PM – Optional sunset viewpoints
